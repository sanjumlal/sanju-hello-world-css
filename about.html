About Kerala Blasters FC

Our Legacy

Kerala Blasters Football Club is a professional football club based in Kochi, Kerala, India. Founded in 2014, the club is one of the most popular and successful football teams in the Indian Super League (ISL). The club has a dedicated fanbase called the "Manjappada" (Yellow Brigade), known for their passionate support at every match.

The Kerala Blasters have established themselves as a major force in Indian football, bringing the game to the forefront of the country's sports culture. With their electrifying performances and memorable matches, the Blasters have become a symbol of hope, pride, and resilience for millions of fans.

Our Vision

At Kerala Blasters, our vision is to bring the people of Kerala together through the love of football. We strive to be a beacon of hope for young talents in the region, providing them with the opportunity to shine on national and international stages. We are committed to building a sustainable and winning team that will represent the spirit of Kerala on and off the field.

We also believe in the power of football to inspire and unite communities. Our mission is to promote the sport at the grassroots level and encourage participation in both the competitive and recreational aspects of football.

Our Achievements

  • Indian Super League Finalists (2014, 2016)
  • Known for a passionate fan base - Manjappada
  • Multiple wins in domestic and international friendlies
  • Consistently ranked among the top teams in ISL
  • Active engagement with youth and community football programs
